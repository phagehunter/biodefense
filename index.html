Executive Summary

US federal investments in biodefense are now declining. While the administration prioritizes biosurveillance, broader and sustained investments are needed to mitigate future outbreaks.

  • Medical Countermeasures: Sustained at $11.78B.
  • Public Health Prep: Significant 32% decrease.
  • Global Engagement: Waning support across State and DoD.
